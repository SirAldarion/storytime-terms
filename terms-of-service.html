What StoryTime is

StoryTime lets adult family members record audio tellings of stories and share them privately within a family space. Recordings are available only to members of that family — StoryTime is not a public platform and has no public sharing features.

Family-only sharing

Recordings are shared with everyone who has joined your family space using your family code. Share the code only with people you trust — like a house key. Anyone in the family can listen to all of the family's recordings.
